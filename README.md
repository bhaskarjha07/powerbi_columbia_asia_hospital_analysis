**🚀 Project Overview**
This project was undertaken as a Data Analyst Consultant for Columbia Asia Hospital to generate actionable insights that support strategic decision-making.
The analysis focuses on evaluating revenue generation, identifying staffing needs, and designing patient discount strategies using data-driven approaches.


**🎯 Business Objectives**
-- Assess hospital revenue performance across departments
-- Identify departments requiring additional staffing
-- Develop effective patient discount strategies
-- Improve overall operational efficiency and profitability


**🧠 Key Business Questions**
-- Which departments generate the highest and lowest revenue?
-- What are the trends in patient inflow across departments?
-- Which departments are understaffed or overburdened?
-- How can discounts be optimized without reducing profitability?
-- What are the key drivers of hospital revenue?


**📊 Data Sources**
-- Patient records
-- Treatment details and billing data
-- Department-wise performance metrics
-- Admission and discharge data


**🛠️ Tools & Technologies**

-- Power BI


**📈 Key Metrics Analyzed**
-- Revenue by department
-- Patient inflow and occupancy rate
-- Average treatment cost
-- Department-wise profitability
-- Discount impact on revenue
-- Resource utilization


**📉 Approach & Methodology**
**1. Data Cleaning & Preprocessing**
-- Handled missing and inconsistent values
-- Standardized patient and billing records
-- Prepared datasets for analysis

**2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**
-- Revenue distribution across departments
-- Patient inflow trends
-- Treatment cost analysis

**3. Revenue Analysis**
-- Identified top revenue-generating departments
-- Highlighted low-performing areas for improvement

**4. Workforce Insights**
-- Analyzed patient load per department
-- Identified departments requiring additional staff

**5. Discount Strategy Analysis**
-- Evaluated impact of discounts on revenue
-- Proposed optimized discount strategies


**📊 Key Insights**
-- A few departments contribute the majority of hospital revenue
-- Certain departments experience high patient load but limited staffing
-- Discounting strategies can be optimized to attract patients while maintaining margins
-- Seasonal trends influence patient inflow


**💡 Recommendations**
-- Increase hiring in high-demand departments
-- Optimize pricing and discount strategies based on patient segments
-- Improve operational efficiency in underperforming departments
-- Monitor key KPIs through dashboards for continuous improvement
